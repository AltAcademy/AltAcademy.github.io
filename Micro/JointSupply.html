Joint Supply

Key Terms
Joint Supply: When two goods are supplied together
Theory

What is joint supply?


Where goods are in joint supply, it means they are supplied together. An increase or decrease in the supply of one good leads to an increase or decrease in the supply of another.




Increase in supply of beef will lead to a rising market supply of beef hides (leather).
